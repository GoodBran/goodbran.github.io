Goodbran is a personal blog about writing, publishing, and sharing notes and piano scores. It's a space for exploring coding, music notation, and creative projects.

## What You'll Find Here

- **Coding Notes**: Thoughts on Rails, deployment, and developer tools
- **Music Scores**: Sheet music created with LilyPond, available as PDFs and playable audio
- **Tutorials**: Step-by-step guides for technical and musical projects

## Tech Stack

This site is built with:
- **Jekyll** - Static site generator
- **Mozilla Protocol Design System** - Clean, professional styling
