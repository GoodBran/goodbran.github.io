Corner was with us from February 2017 to May 2026.

She got her name because, when I first met her, she was very afraid of people. She would hide inside the corner of the sofa, watching the world from the safest place she could find. So she became Corner.

Over time, that little hiding place became part of her story, not all of it. She grew into a quiet, familiar presence at home, the kind that made ordinary days feel softer.

Goodbye, Corner. Thank you for the years, the trust, and the gentle company.
